DTB Guidelines
The DTB is calculated on each day by computing the average amount of water each animal worked for on the previous 7 testing days (mean-2*std), divided by the animal's daily weight. For the DTB, as described in the policy document, this is then clipped to a minimum value of 10.0 ml/kg. In our lab, our protocol allows for the DTB to be disregarded (ie, animals fluid intake can drop below the DTB and both the lab and VCS will monitor animals more closely for problems. Weekly weight and health summaries are provided to VCS in compliance with this procedure.
